## v1.0.0 - Initial release

QueueCast Planner v1.0.0 delivers explainable forecasting and capacity simulation for fictional service operations.

## Included

- Three synthetic queues with 12 historical weeks each.
- Least-squares trend and residual uncertainty over an eight-week horizon.
- Five adjustable scenario assumptions: demand, agents, productivity, absence, and service buffer.
- Effective capacity, buffered need, weekly gap, utilization, risk, required agents, and plan score.
- Canvas planning chart, KPI strip, weekly detail table, and quantified staffing brief.
- Explicit human adopt or reject gate with local event history.
- Privacy-safe JSON export with no worker schedule or personal data.
- Logic, repository, local browser, deployed browser, privacy, HTTP, desktop, mobile, keyboard, and failure evidence.

## Scope

Forecasts are deterministic estimates from synthetic data. No schedule, worker message, employment decision, or external action is created.
